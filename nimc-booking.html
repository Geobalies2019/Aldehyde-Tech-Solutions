What You Need
to Bring

To successfully obtain your NIN, you must present the appropriate certificate from the National Population Commission (NPC).

🧑‍💼

Adults

NPC Attestation Certificate — Required for all adult applicants (18 years and above).

👶

Children Born in Nigeria

NPC Birth Certificate — Required for minors born within Nigeria.

🌍

Children Born Outside Nigeria

NPC Birth Notification — Required for Nigerian children born abroad.

🪪

Valid ID — All Applicants

Bring a valid government-issued Passport or Driving License to your appointment.

⚠️

NPC certificates are obtainable from the official NPC website at https://nationalpopulation.gov.ng/. Your NIN cannot be issued without the appropriate NPC certificate.

Fees

Transparent Pricing

Two payments are required. Pay NIMC online in advance and settle the processing fee after successful NIN issuance.

$74

NIMC Fee

Paid directly to NIMC online before your appointment. This is a mandatory government fee.

Processing Fee

Paid after your NIN is successfully processed. Covers in-person assistance and document handling.
